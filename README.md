---

## About SECP

The **Securities and Exchange Commission of Pakistan (SECP)** is the apex regulatory body responsible for regulating:

- Corporate sector
- Capital markets
- Insurance companies
- Non-banking finance companies

Established under the **Securities and Exchange Commission of Pakistan Act, 1997**, SECP plays a pivotal role in fostering a transparent, efficient, and fair corporate environment in Pakistan.
